Spicy Arrabiata Penne

Easy, tasty, and quick for 6-person family.

Check other recipes
1

− Ingredients −

  1. 1 pound penne rigate (penne pasta with ridges)
  2. One 26.42-ounce container chopped tomatoes
  3. 1/4 cup olive oil
  4. 6 leaves fresh basil, chopped
  5. 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  6. 1/2 teaspoon red chile flakes
  7. Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
2

− Directions −

Boil water.Add kosher salt to the boiling water, then add the pasta.Cook 9 minutes
09:00
Add the olive oil and heat until the oil starts to shimmer. Add the garlic and cook, stirring, until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes. Add the chopped tomatoes,salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil and cook for 5 minutes.
Add the chopped basil.Drain the pasta and add sauce and basil.
