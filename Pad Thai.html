Pad Thai

Quick, easy and worth making for couple.

Check other recipes
1

− Ingredients −

  1. 1 (8 ounce) package dried flat rice noodles
  2. 8 ounces medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
  3. 2 eggs, beaten
  4. 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  5. 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  6. 8 ounces skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1 inch cubes
  7. 6 green onions, chopped into 1 inch pieces
2

− Directions −

Fill a large bowl with hot tap water and place the noodles in it to soak for 20 minutes.
20:00
Stir together lime juice, onions, oyster sauce, 2 teaspoons of the chile sauce and chicken stock. Set aside.
Cook and stir the eggs until firm. Add shrimp and chicken; cook, stirring constantly until shrimp is opaque and chicken is cooked through
Add the chopped basil.Drain the pasta and add sauce and basil.
