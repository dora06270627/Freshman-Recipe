Easy Breaded Shrimp

Easy, good, and wonderful for a family dinner.

Check other recipes
1

− Ingredients −

  1. 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  2. 4 cups shrimp, peeled and deveined
  3. 1 egg, beaten
  4. 2 cups dry bread crumbs
2

− Directions −

Heat oil in a large skillet. Dip the shrimp in the egg, then coat the shrimp with bread crumbs. Fry the shrimp in the hot oil.
